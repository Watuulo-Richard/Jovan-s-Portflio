Senior Statistician

Lubega Jovan is a highly skilled Senior Statistician working under the Ministry of Education and Sports in Uganda. With extensive experience in statistical analysis, data collection, and interpretation, Jovan has made significant contributions to various educational and sports initiatives within the country. As a senior member of the team, he plays a crucial role in overseeing statistical research projects, ensuring accurate data reporting, and providing insights that inform decision-making processes. Through his work, Jovan has contributed to improving the effectiveness of programs in the Ministry, enhancing the quality of education, and promoting sports development in Uganda. His commitment to precision and innovation in statistical work has had a lasting impact on policy formulation and the success of key projects.
